DESCRIPTION

So you know a thing or two about sugar cookies but want to get a full understanding of how to ice and decorate cookies? Well then this Royal icing Beginner bouquet class is for you! You will learn how to ice like a pro and how to create various piping techniques.

We will provide you with the sugar cookies, show you how to achieve the perfect consistencies of Royal icingg

Included in the class:

  • A box of 12 sugar cookies
  • Piping bags with different colours of royal icing
  • Demonstration on how to make Royal icing
  • Demonstration on how to pipe, flood, ice and decorate your cookies
  • Sprinkles, glitter, fondant will be provided to decorate your cookies
  • Recipe card of our Royal icing, with the secret ingredient
  • Recipe card of our exclusive Sugar cookie recipe
  • Advice on how to store, package and present your cookies
  • Advice on how to start up a cookie business
  • Light snack and beverage
  • In class assistance and after-class WhatsApp assistance
